Audio & Video

Commonly supported formats on the Web

MP3

  • In HTML: type="audio/mp3"
  • Pros: It's size is reduced. 1/10 of data CDs
  • Cons: It's quality may deteriorate during the encoding

OGG

  • In HTML: type="audio/ogg"
  • Pros: Potential for tunning
  • Cons: Harder to decode than MP3

WAV

  • In HTML: type="audio/wav"
  • Pros: You get the best audio available
  • Cons: The files may take a big amount of space

MP4

  • In HTML: type="video/mp4"
  • Pros: Standard on Web
  • Cons: Hard time encoding and decoding

WebM

  • In HTML: type="video/webm"
  • Pros: Higher quality videos
  • Cons: Less support available

OGG

  • In HTML: type="video/ogg"
  • Pros: Patent free, giving you more options
  • Cons: Partially supported
